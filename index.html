About SVJ Packaging

Your Partner in Packaging Since 2019

Founded in Orange County, California, SVJ Packaging was built on a simple idea: brands deserve a single partner that can handle the full packaging lifecycle — from design through delivery.

Over the years, we've grown into a trusted name for some of the biggest brands in the hemp and CBD space. Our team combines deep industry knowledge with a global production network and local fulfillment capabilities to deliver packaging solutions that are on-brand, on-time, and on-budget.

We work with companies nationwide, bringing the same hands-on approach and attention to detail to every project, no matter the scale.
